---
title: "Pay a Bill"
description: "Pay a subscription bill for DStv, GOtv, Zuku, or StarTimes."
---

The request first checks that your account has sufficient balance. If successful, an order is placed. A [callback URL](/callback) is required to receive the final status; you can also poll the [status](/bills/status) endpoint.

**POST** `/v1/bus/order/{service}`

---

## Services

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `dstv` | DStv |
| `gotv` | GOtv |
| `zuku` | Zuku |
| `startimes` | StarTimes |

---

## Request

<RequestExample>
```bash cURL
curl -X POST https://api.tupay.africa/v1/bus/order/dstv \
  -H 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  -H 'Authorization: Bearer {token}' \
  -d '{
    "account":   "0733000000",
    "amount":    10,
    "currency":  "KES",
    "reference": "ref1234",
    "name":      "John",
    "phone":     "254722000000",
    "shortcode": "100100"
  }'
```
</RequestExample>

<ParamField body="account" type="string" required>
  Subscription account number.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="amount" type="number" required>
  Amount to pay.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="currency" type="string" required>
  Currency code.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="reference" type="string">
  Your internal reference.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="name" type="string">
  Customer first name.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="phone" type="string">
  Customer phone number.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="shortcode" type="string">
  Shortcode if required by the provider.
</ParamField>

---
